Intro

For almost a decade I have been providing technical solutions in a variety of environments. After working in Audio Visual support for meetings and events in hotels I’ve now transitioned to the role of Web Developer where I bring the same level of proactive service while working with projects from start to finish.

For many years in the hospitality industry I have had the opportunity to provide top notch customer service through technical solutions that connect attendees and leave them inspired. This experience has ingrained in me the desire to look for ways to efficiently solve problems while achieving impactful results that leave a lasting impression.

As I’ve transitioned to a career in software development I now provide the same level of commitment to quality end results through attention to detail, dedication to the customer’s goals, and troubleshooting results. My approach to collaborating with a team is through empathetic understanding of the objectives and embracing each new challenge.

Striving daily to improve the world around me by focusing on what I can control, whether in work, parenting, or ultimate frisbee with friends, I hope to leave those I interact with in a better position.

Work/Projects

PERSONAL PROJECT - 2021

Fellow Actualize alumni Pol Subjanajouy and I worked together on a React and Ruby on Rails project called Monster Mashup. We started with a React tutorial that built a meme generator and utilized Pol's unique idea and illustration skills to create an exquisite corpse style app.

ACTUALIZE - 2020

During this 17 week coding bootcamp I covered the basics of fullstack web development while focusing on enhancing our problem solving skills, and expanding our troubleshooting toolset to prepare for a career in the world of tech.

Capstone Project - Bird Spotting App: web app that allows users to login, determine common birds seen in current location based on State and County, save a bird watching session and sightings, view previous sessions and other users sessions. Integrated eBird API, Unsplash API, and MapBox Geolocator API into a Ruby on Rails backend, Vue.js frontend, and a Bootstrap theme.

PSAV 2011-Present

PSAV serves as a third-party in-house audio visual provider in hotels around the world with support for audio, video, lighting, and scenic elements for events including professional conferences, awards dinners, galas, and meetings.

Since 2014 I have been a Director of Event Technology. In this role I lead a team at a hotel, overseeing all aspects of our operation. Beginning with the sales process I support our sales team in designing a technical solution that will meet the needs of our clients while being mindful of their timeline and budget. I then manage the project as we assemble the necessary gear and labor. When the event date nears I oversee the setup, operation, and strike of the events while interfacing with the client once they’re onsite. Providing technical solutions for a complex range of clients on a daily basis.

About

After growing up in the woods of western Pennsylvania I traversed through several states and career dreams during and after college before settling in Chicago where I began working for an audio visual company that works primarily in hotels. The seed was planted for a career in software development during my first semester of college, but was not cultivated until over a decade later when I took the leap into an intensive coding bootcamp.

When not working I look for any excuse to be outside with my family. From hiking, camping, frisbee (ultimate and golf), any day spent outside is a good day. I also enjoy dabbling on a guitar and catching live concerts when I can.
